Our company, The Guitar Store, is a locally owned and operated business based in the heart of Music City – Nashville, TN. We specialize in crafting high-quality, hand-made guitars for musicians of all skill levels.

Our team of experienced luthiers have a passion for the craft and strive to create instruments that not only sound great but are also visually stunning. We use only the finest woods and materials, and each guitar is carefully inspected and adjusted to ensure optimal playability. We offer a wide variety of guitar models, including acoustics, electrics, and basses. Whether you're a professional musician or just starting out, we have something for everyone. In addition to our standard models, we also offer custom builds and personalized setups to help you achieve your desired sound.

We pride ourselves on providing excellent customer service and are always available to answer any questions or address any concerns. We believe that every musician deserves to play an instrument they love, and we're dedicated to helping you find the perfect guitar for your needs.

In addition to our guitar sales, we also offer repair and maintenance services, including restringing, setup, and fretwork. Our shop is fully equipped to handle any repair needs you may have.
